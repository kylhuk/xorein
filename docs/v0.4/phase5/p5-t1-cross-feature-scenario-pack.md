# Phase 5 · P5-T1 Cross-Feature Scenario Pack

## Purpose
Describe the positive, negative, and recovery scenarios that prove the v0.4 scope for `P1-T*` through `P4-T*` and feed into `VA-X1` evidence.

## Contract
- Every scenario maps to at least one validation artifact in Phases 1–4 and specifies the expected reason taxonomy output described in Section 1.3 of `TODO_v04.md`.
- Recovery scenarios enumerate deterministic state transitions for appeals, rollback triggers, and bypass paths, ensuring evidence is available for `VA-M6` and `VA-X2`.
- The pack includes a compatibility summary that tracks the status of open decisions listed in Section 8 and references the release checklist artifacts in `p5-t3-release-gate-handoff.md`.
